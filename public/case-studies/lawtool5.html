LawTool5 - Case Study

AI-assisted legal document analysis platform for extracting clauses, summarizing contracts, and finding relevant precedents.

Problem

Legal teams spend high effort reviewing large contracts and searching across scattered historical documents.

Solution

Built an NLP-first workflow with OCR ingestion, semantic retrieval, and collaborative annotation for legal researchers.

Architecture

Document processing pipeline, transformer-based embeddings, search index, RBAC-protected API, and React review UI.

Impact

Accelerated contract review cycles and improved precedent discovery quality through semantic ranking.
