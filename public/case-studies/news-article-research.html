News Article Research - Case Study

Pipeline and analytics product for ingesting articles, extracting insights, and surfacing trends with semantic search.

Problem

News intelligence workflows were fragmented, forcing manual reading and weak trend detection across large article volumes.

Solution

Designed ingestion + NLP pipelines and exposed processed data through a FastAPI backend with an interactive React interface.

Architecture

Scheduled scraping, NLP enrichment, FAISS/Elasticsearch similarity indexing, dashboard UI, and containerized CI/CD jobs.

Impact

Improved content discovery speed and enabled faster editorial/research decisions with cluster-based trend visibility.
